ArcGIS Downstream Impact for US K-12 Schools

Search for your ArcGIS Online Organization prefix (must be a U.S. K12 account). The prefix is the part of the URL in bold:
http://myschoolname.maps.arcgis.com



Examples:
  • Education (Esri)
  • Ed Resources (Esri)
  • Fairfax HS
If the school is not in this system or you entered the prefix incorrectly, you'll receive a rather unfortunate 404 error. We're keeping things simple here.

Why isn't my school listed?
ADI attempts to document school-based ArcGIS Online organizations with "anonymous access" enabled (image).

All data on these pages were obtained without login (public access).
